Why sponsor?

Sponsor the Swiss Raku and Perl Miniconf to get a real community audience for Raku and Perl related topics and present your company or association. Apart from the sponsorship goodies below, you will get in touch with the Raku and Perl community directly and meet the developers face to face.

If you would like to consider joining the list of current sponsors then please take a look at our sponsorship prospectus.

What could you sponsor?

You could sponsor any of the following items:

  • Attendees dinner
  • Drinks & catering during the event
  • Travel & hotel costs for international visitors
  • T-shirts or coffee cups
  • Conference dinner and drinks

Or you could become a general sponsor. We have three packages:

Bronze | CHF 300

  • Link & logo on our website
  • Logo included on video recordings of talks
  • Mentions during the opening and closing speeches
  • Mentions in blog posts on blogs.perl.org

Silver | CHF 500

  • Bronze Sponsor package and ...
  • The option of displaying promotional material at the venue
  • Free business tariff attendance for 1 person

Gold | CHF 1000

  • Bronze + Silver Sponsor package and ...
  • 2 more business attendees
  • 5 minute presentation of your business

We are open to discussing other benefits for our sponsors please don't hesitate to contact us.
