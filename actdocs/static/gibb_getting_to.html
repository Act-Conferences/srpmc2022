Coordinates

See here for the coordinates to the venue.

By Air

From Zurich Airport to Bern by train takes 1:30 hours.

From Geneva Airport to Bern by train takes 2 hours.

From EuroAirport Basel, Mulhouse, Freiburg to Bern by train takes 1:30 hours.

By Train

The main train station Bern, Hauptbahnhof (Bern, Main station) is just 10 minutes by foot from the venue.

You can find timetables and purchase train tickets in advance on the SBB site.

Note that Swiss train ticket pricing is eminently sensible, you will pay the same price no matter when you buy the ticket or what time of day you take the train.

By Bus

If you stay at a Hotel they will provide you with a free Bus ticket for your stay. You can find timetables and purchase bus tickets at the same website as for trains: SBB. You also can buy tickets from machines at each station.

From the main station you take Bus NFO 20. The departure is a little hidden behind the Restaurant Tibits at the main entry. There is just one direction (Wankdorf), so don't worry. Just look for bus number 20.

The bus stop next to the venue is called Bern, Gewerbeschule. It's just to bus stops from the main station.

By Foot

At main station ask the direction for Lorraine Quartier (district Lorraine). You will be pointed to descend a bit along the main station. After 5 minutes walk you will see a huge bridge, crossing a river. Cross the bridge, enjoy the view, after the bridge the venue is on the campus at your left.

Please contact us by Email if you have any questions or problems.
